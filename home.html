Why Tio?

About Me

Have a great day at work with Tio!

Do you ever feel like giving up on a work day because it's too much to handle or too much to digest? Don't worry!
Tio is here to save your day and add some glitter! Tio is special chatBot built to help reduce stress in corporate world.
If you feel like you are a pressure cooker because of your boss nagging or getting annoyed by your whistling co-worker or feel like smashing your phone after rejecting you wife's 17th missed call, Tio is ready to give you a shoulder!

Why Tio?

Story

The chatbot is inspired by Key's solo song 'Good and Great' released in 2023. The song is about employees trying to deal with the corporate world and ace in the competition. So I thought why not build a chatBot to ease someone's hectic day at work and make them smile a bit? And thus Tio was created!

Mission

Tio aims to comfort and cheer up people at work. It wants to help people have a good work life balance and enjoy work at the most! The various features of Tio are designed to cater the timely moods of employees. whenever, whatever Tio is ready to give employees a shoulder to rest on. And the best part is that every pixel of this app is free!

Vision

We wish to comfort employees using Tio in the long run as well! Our vision is to add more features and train the bot for better outcomes. Our future plans include adding To-Do lists and reminders. And including other integrations such as Spotify, Youtube and more! Please show love to Tio in the future too!
